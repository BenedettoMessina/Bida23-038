Must Watch TV Shows

The West Wing

"The West Wing" is a political drama series that offers an inside look into the lives of the staff working in the West Wing of the White House. It follows their professional and personal challenges as they navigate.

Arrow

"Arrow" is a superhero television series that follows the story of Oliver Queen, a billionaire playboy who becomes a vigilante archer after being stranded on a remote island for five years. Returning to his city the green Arrow.

The Big Bang Theory

"The Big Bang Theory" is a sitcom centered around a group of socially awkward scientists and their interactions with each other and the world around them. The show follows physicists Leonard and Sheldon, along friends.

Vampire Diaries

"The Vampire Diaries" is a supernatural drama series set in the fictional town of Mystic Falls. It follows the lives of vampires, werewolves, witches, and humans.

Two and a Half Man

"Two and a Half Men" is a sitcom that revolves around the lives of two brothers, Charlie and Alan Harper, and Alan's son, Jake. The show follows their comedic misadventures as they navigate relationships, family dynamics.

ER

"ER" is a medical drama series that provides an intense and realistic portrayal of the emergency room staff at County General Hospital in Chicago. The show follows the lives of doctors.
